What is this site?

What is this site?

It's a place to find pair programming partners. Want to build a project with someone else? Want to pay it forward and help someone out? Need a mentor? We help you find like-minded developers.

How do I use it?

How do I use it?

Signup for an account and you'll be able to submit Posts which are mini-proposals telling people what you want to work on. Anyone who's interested can message you directly and then you can organize from there.

You can also look around in the Friend Finder for people who work on the same kind of stuff. You'll have to send them a friend request, but after you'll be able to message each other and figure out what you wanna work on.

What about my privacy?!?!?

What about my privacy?!?!?

You need to submit your email to signup, but other users, even friends, will never have access to any of your personal information

If you want to put your website/github/email address in your profile or posting, go ahead! Just be aware that all registered members will be able to see it.
